About ICEE

Get ready to connect with like-minded college students from around the state, learn from entrepreneurs who started their own businesses in college, be inspired, and have fun!

The Iowa College Entrepreneurs Event (ICEE) is planned and executed by college students from UNI for college students all around Iowa. The purpose of ICEE is to connect college entrepreneurs from around the state and help them establish relationships with other students who have a common goal to pursue entrepreneurship in the future.

We can't wait to see you on October 12th at the Iowa College Entrepreneurs Event!
